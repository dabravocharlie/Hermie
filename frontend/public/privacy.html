Hermie ("we", "us") is a personal-finance organizer that helps you understand your money. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. Questions: [YOUR_EMAIL].

Information we collect

Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address through our authentication provider, Clerk.

Financial information you enter. Hermie stores what you choose to add: income, bills and expenses, investment holdings, watchlist symbols, notes, and calendar reminders. You control this data and can delete it in the app.

Assistant conversations. Messages you send to the Hermie assistant are stored so it can remember your conversation.

We do not ask for or store bank logins, card numbers, Social Security numbers, or brokerage credentials.

How we use your information

To operate the app: show your cash flow and portfolio, power reminders, and let the Hermie assistant answer questions grounded in your data. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Service providers we share data with

We use a small set of providers to run Hermie. Each receives only what it needs:

  • Clerk — sign-in and account management (receives your email/authentication data).
  • Render — application hosting and the database where your data is stored (United States).
  • Vercel — hosting for the app's interface.
  • Finnhub — stock prices and company news. We send ticker symbols only, not personal information.
  • Anthropic — powers the Hermie assistant. When you chat with Hermie, we send your message and a snapshot of your financial data (amounts, holdings, etc.) so it can respond. This is processed to generate replies.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept until you delete it or close your account. You can remove individual items in the app. To delete your account and associated data, contact [YOUR_EMAIL].

Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored with our hosting provider. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children

Hermie is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Not financial advice

Hermie is an educational tool. It explains concepts and your own numbers; it does not provide personalized financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. See our Terms of Use.

Changes
